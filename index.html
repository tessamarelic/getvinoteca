✦ Features

Everything your
cellar needs

From the moment you scan a label to the perfect pour — Vinoteca handles it all.

📷

Instant Label Scanning

Point your camera at any wine label. Anthropic Vision AI reads the name, vintage, varietal, region and drinking window instantly — no typing required.

✦

AI Sommelier

Ask anything. Get personalised recommendations drawn directly from your own cellar. Pairing dinner, planning an occasion, or deciding what to open tonight.

⏳

Peak Drinking Windows

Every bottle gets a drinking window and peak period. Vinoteca alerts you when bottles are at their best — so you never miss the perfect moment.

🛒

Should I Buy This?

Standing in a bottle shop? Scan any label and ask your AI Sommelier for an instant verdict — Yes, Maybe or Pass — based on your taste, your cellar and the price.

💰

Live Market Prices

Automatic market price lookups show what your cellar is worth today and where to find the best deals on bottles you love.

📊

Collection Insights

Beautiful charts show your collection by type, vintage and region. Understand your drinking habits and discover what your cellar says about your taste.

📥

Easy Import

Already have a spreadsheet? Import your entire collection in seconds. Vinoteca recognises common column names and maps them automatically.
